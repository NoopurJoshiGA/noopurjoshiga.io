About me

I’m fueled by ramen, matcha, Indian chai, meditation and ...good code

My flair for problem solving and fascination for technology led me to taking a Computer Science degree during university. After graduating, I spent couple of years in a technical / client-facing hybrid role, which allowed me to not only practice the software development process but also to understand the client’s requirements.

As of June, I enrolled in General Assembly’s Web Development Immersive course to develop my skills further and dive deeper into programming.

My time at General Assembly taught me that perseverance, curiosity and a logical approach to problem solving is key to strive as a Web Developer.

I’m looking for opportunities to work as a Full Stack Developer where I can translate my education and experience into an agile and creative workplace.
